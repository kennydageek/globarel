Services

Services includes but not
limited to these big 5

01

Landing in Canada and Accommodation

One of the major challenges for new student landing in Canada is the ability to settle in fast. The most important part will be accommodation. We assist clients in getting accommodation before they land. This helps them settle in and integrate fastest. We also arrange for airport pickup at the request of the client.

02

School Admission

We assist potential candidate in securing admissions in Canadian schools, provide advisory service on course of study and support the student through the admission process. This would involve you appointing B&O Consult Inc as your representative for securing admission in Canadian schools

03

Visa Application

Once the admission processs is completed, we also provide advisory service for visa application based on request by client

04

Custodian for minors

This is only applicable to unaccompanied students under 18 years: we support for students, provide consistent report to parents and represent parents when required. Please note that parental consent is required for this service.

05

Employment opportunities

For graduates, we help them build their resume and also prepare them for interviews. We partner with Human Resources expert to assist with job search
